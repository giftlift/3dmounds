Mounds in 3d!

This is a single tile of lidar data, processed with open source tools.
The location is just above The Plains in Athens County, Ohio.
It is presented here with a high quality color image overlay and a hillshade layer which is better for viewing the ground features.
There appears to be a earthen henge in the field and a mound on the hill.
It may take a while to load. Once loaded, click “help” for a list of features.
For touchscreen: two fingers zoom and one finger rotates. For mouse: left click rotates and scroll zooms. "Shift + S" saves a snapshot.
